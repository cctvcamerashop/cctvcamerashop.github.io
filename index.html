Types of CCTV Cameras

Dome Cameras

Dome cameras are ideal for indoor and outdoor use. Their dome shape makes them less noticeable and provides a wide field of view.

Bullet Cameras

Bullet cameras are highly visible and act as a deterrent to potential intruders. They are suitable for long-distance viewing and outdoor surveillance.

PTZ Cameras

PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras allow you to remotely control the camera's movement and zoom. They are perfect for monitoring large areas.

Wireless Cameras

Wireless cameras offer flexible installation options as they don't require wired connections. They are ideal for temporary setups and remote locations.

Infrared Cameras

Infrared cameras provide clear images in low-light and complete darkness. They are perfect for 24/7 surveillance.

Benefits of CCTV Cameras

CCTV cameras are essential for maintaining security and safety in various environments. Here are some of the key benefits:

  • Deterrence: Visible CCTV cameras can deter criminal activity.
  • Evidence Collection: Recorded footage can be used as evidence in investigations.
  • Remote Monitoring: Monitor your property from anywhere using mobile apps and internet connectivity.
  • Peace of Mind: Knowing your property is under surveillance provides peace of mind.
